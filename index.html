About Us

Welcome to the Foothill Engineering Club! We are a community of students passionate about engineering, technology, and innovation. Our mission is to provide hands-on experience and foster collaboration among aspiring engineers at Foothill College.

Our Projects

We engage in a variety of projects that allow members to apply their skills and learn new technologies. Some of our current and past projects include:

  • Robotics Challenge: Designing and building robots for intercollegiate competitions.
  • Solar Car Initiative: Developing a solar-powered vehicle prototype.
  • 3D Printing Workshop: Creating models and prototypes using 3D printing technology.
